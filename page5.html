What We Believe

WE BELIEVE
The Bible is the inspired, infallible (unaltered) WORD OF GOD written by Holy men of old as they were moved by the Holy Ghost.

WE BELIEVE 
In one God who is infinite in power, holy in nature, attributes and purpose, omniscient and omnipresent. WE BELIEVE that He is revealed to us as the FATHER in creation, SON in redemption and HOLY SPIRIT in His comforting grace that never leaves those who trust Him.

WE BELIEVE
That in Jesus Christ dewelleth all the fullness of the GODHEAD bodily for it pleased the FATHER that in Him should all the fullness dwell.

WE BELIEVE
In the virgin birth, that JESUS was both HUMAN and DIVINE-GOD in the flesh.

WE BELIEVE
In the DEATH, BURIAL, AND RESSURECTION OF JESUS. WE BELIEVE that He ascended on High and sent His Spirit, the Holy Ghost, who was poured out over Jerusalem over 2000 years ago and is still filing the hearts of those who diligently seek Him today.

WE BELIEVE
In the baptism in water by immersion in the name of the Lord Jesus Christ for the REMISSION of SINS, and the Baptism of the HOLY GHOST, SPEAKING in TONGUES as the SPIRIT gives UTTERANCE. This constitutes the new birth.

WE BELIEVE
In a HOLY and SANCTIFIED life, divine healing, communion, foot washing, and the glorious catching away (THE RAPTURE) of the Saints when JESUS returns in the cloud of glory to take His people to Heaven!
